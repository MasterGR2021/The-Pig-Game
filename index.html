Rules

  • Choose one of the two players (i.e. Player 1 or Player 2)
  • On your turn, you can roll a dice and the number that appears on the dice will be added to your current score.
  • You can roll a dice as many time you until a one appears on the dice.
  • If a one appears on the dice then your current score will reset back to zero.
  • On each roll of dice, you can either do another roll or pass it to next player.
  • Whoever reaches to 100 points first will win the game.
